Admission Guide

Shortlist correctly, verify requirements, and apply through the right portal (uni-assist or direct).

1) Shortlist with the right sources

Use DAAD’s programme search/degree databases; then verify on the university programme page.

Official links DAAD: All degree programmes DAAD: International programmes

2) Check eligibility

Before spending time applying, run checks through DAAD Admission Database and related tools.

Official link DAAD: Admission Database

3) APS (India) — do early

APS verifies your documents. It becomes your timeline bottleneck if you start late.

Official links APS India APS checklists APS FAQs

4) Apply: uni-assist vs direct

If your university is a uni-assist member, follow the official steps and apply through My assist.

Official links uni-assist (official) How to apply My assist portal
