After Landing (First Week)

Focus order: SIM → groceries → Anmeldung → Tax ID → transport → university onboarding.

Anmeldung (address registration)

Register your address with the local office (rules and availability vary by city).

Official links Bundesportal: Wohnsitz anmelden Online registration (where available)

Tax ID (IdNr.)

You’ll need it for payroll, taxes and many formal processes.

Official link BZSt: Tax ID

Rundfunkbeitrag (broadcast contribution)

Per dwelling contribution; in a WG typically one account for the apartment.

Official links English overview Register

Deutschlandticket (local/regional transport)

Check your city’s transport association and ticket rules.

Official links Bundesregierung: Deutschlandticket DB: Deutschlandticket
